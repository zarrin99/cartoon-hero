Frieren

About Frieren

Friren is the main character from the anime Sousou no Frieren, she is from the race of Elves and she was in the hero's party that was able to kill the demon race's king

Frieren and the hero's party

Somethings About Friren

  1. Frieren is oblivious about time since elves live for a long long time compared to humans.
  2. Friren loves magic and loves collecting magic that does almost anything. for example she collected a magic that is able to get rid of rust off of any metal surfaces.
  3. Friren is pretty laid back for things other than magic, but she is an extremely powerful magician.

Friren's Friends

Most of the members from the hero's party have either passed away or retired due to old age. Frieren's current adventure party consist of her friend's apprentices who are her students now.

Friren and her current adventurer's party

  • Himmel: the hero
  • Eisen: the warrior
